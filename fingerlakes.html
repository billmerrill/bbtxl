Ben Schultz again planned a beautiful tour of the Finger Lakes and the surrounding parts of upstate New York. It started in Jamestown on 2014/08/03 and ended in Fair Haven on 2014/08/09. There's additional route details on the cue sheets.

Day 1: Jamestown to Ellicotville

Day 2: Ellicotville to Buffalo

Day 3: Buffalo to Rochester

Day 4: Rochester to Keuka

Day 5: Keuka to Trumansburg

Day 6: Trumansburg to Auburn

Day 7: Auburn to Fair Haven
