Dave Helder planned this trip. We wanted to see some giant redwood trees and finish the trip by crossing the Golden Gate Bridge. The first half of the trip is based off of the Adventure Cycling Association Pacific Coast (section 3). It started in Trinidad on 2015/06/08 and ended in San Francisco on 2015/06/13.

Day 1: Trinidad to Fortuna

Lunch: Lost Coast Brewery

Dinner: Ell River Brewing

Camping: Riverwalk RV Park

Day 2: Fortuna to Richardson Grove

This day passes through the Avenue of the Giants, with plenty of roadside attractions.

Camping: Richardson Grove State Park

Day 3: Richardson Grove to Fort Bragg

This day includes the Legget Hill climb. If you missed your chance to bike through a Redwood on day 2, you get a second chance on this day.

Camping: MacKerricher State Park

Day 4: Fort Bragg to Booneville

Dinner: Anderson Valley Brewing Company

Camping: Mendocino County Fair RV Park

Day 5: Booneville to Santa Rosa

Lunch: Bear Republic

Dinner: Russian River Brewing Co

Camping: Sonoma County Regional Parks

Day 6: Santa Rosa to San Francisco

Lunch: Lagunitas Brewing Company

Dinner: ... anywhere you'd like in San Francisco

Overnight: Chez Poulet (now closed)
