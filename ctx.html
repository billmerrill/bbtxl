Eric Irrgang planned this trip. Pretty much every day started off with breakfast tacos and had at least one BBQ meal. Like the NE trip, this trip was a loop. We started in Austin on 2017/04/09 and returned on 2017/04/15.

Day 1: Austin to Lockhart

Breakfast: Juan in a Million

Lunch: Independence Brewing

Dinner: Lockhart BBQ potluck; if you do one thing, try a beef rib from Black's Barbecue

Day 2: Lockhart to Canyon Lake

Lunch: Faust Hotel & Brewing Co

Dinner: Brewster's Pizza (now closed)

Camping: Canyon Lake Cabins & Cottages

Day 3: Canyon Lake to Fredericksburg

Lunch: Old 300 BBQ

Dinner: Fredericksburg Brewing Co

Camping: Lady Bird Johnson Municipal Park

Day 4: Fredericksburg to Enchanted Rock

This is a short day, but there's plenty to see at Enchanted Rock!

Breakfast: Old German Bakery & Restaurant

Camping: Enchanted Rock State Natural Area

Day 5: Enchanted Rock to Inks Lake

Lunch: Cooper's Old Time Pit BBQ

Dinner: Hoover's Valley Cafe

Camping: Inks Lake State Park

Day 6: Inks Lake to Austin Burbs

Lunch: Double Horn Brewing Company

Dinner: Proof & Cooper

Camping: Rock Dog Campground (now closed)

Day 7: Austin Burbs to Austin Proper

Breakfast: H12 Outdoor Cafe (now closed)

Lunch: Jester King Brewery (they technically don't have food, but you can order delivery)

Ice cream: Amy's Ice Cream

Dinner: ... anywhere in Austin. I think I went to Torchy's (don't hate).
